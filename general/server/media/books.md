# Books

The most common organization scheme for books is separation by Audiobook then by Author.

```txt
Books
├── Audiobooks
│   ├── Author
│   │   ├── Book1.flac
│   │   └── Book2.flac
│   └── Book
│       ├── Chapter1.flac
│       └── Chapter2.flac
└── Books
    └── Author
        ├── Book1.epub
        └── Book2.mp3
```
